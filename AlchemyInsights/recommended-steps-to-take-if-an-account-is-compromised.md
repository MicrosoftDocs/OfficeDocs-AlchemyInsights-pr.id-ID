[<span data-ttu-id="dd805-103">VIDEO: Memperbaiki akun Office 365 yang dibobol</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dd805-103">VIDEO: Fixing a compromised Office 365 account</span></span>](https://www.microsoft.com/videoplayer/embed/RE2jvOb?pid=ocpVideo0-innerdiv-oneplayer&amp;postJsllMsg=true&amp;maskLevel=20&amp;autoplay=true)
  
1. <span data-ttu-id="dd805-104">[Atur ulang kata sandi pengguna](https://support.office.com/article/7a5d073b-7fae-4aa5-8f96-9ecd041aba9c) segera.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dd805-104">[Reset the user's password](https://support.office.com/article/7a5d073b-7fae-4aa5-8f96-9ecd041aba9c) immediately.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="dd805-105">Jangan bagikan kata sandi Anda yang baru melalui email ke pengguna akhir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dd805-105">Do not communicate the new password through email to the end user.</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="dd805-106">Hapus semua [alamat penerusan](https://support.office.com/article/ab5eb117-0f22-4fa7-a662-3a6bdb0add74) yang mencurigakan di kotak surat.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dd805-106">Remove any suspicious [forwarding addresses](https://support.office.com/article/ab5eb117-0f22-4fa7-a662-3a6bdb0add74) set at the mailbox level.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="dd805-107">Hapus semua [aturan kotak masuk](https://support.office.com/article/1433E3A0-7FB0-4999-B536-50E05CB67FED) yang mencurigakan di kotak surat.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dd805-107">Remove any suspicious [inbox rules](https://support.office.com/article/1433E3A0-7FB0-4999-B536-50E05CB67FED) set within the mailbox.</span></span>

4. <span data-ttu-id="dd805-108">Jika pengguna diblokir untuk mengirimkan email, [masuk ke Pengguna Terbatas untuk membuka blokir akun](https://protection.office.com/?hash=/restrictedusers).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dd805-108">If the user is blocked from sending email, [go to the Restricted Users to unblock the account](https://protection.office.com/?hash=/restrictedusers).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="dd805-109">Begitu selesai, Anda akan dapat melanjutkan pengiriman pesan dalam 1 jam.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dd805-109">Once done, the user should be able to resume sending messages within 1 hour.</span></span>

5. <span data-ttu-id="dd805-110">Hapus akun pengguna dari semua [grup peran administratif](https://support.office.com/article/eac4d046-1afd-4f1a-85fc-8219c79e1504) sampai Anda yakin bahwa akun sudah aman.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dd805-110">Remove the user account from any [administrative role groups](https://support.office.com/article/eac4d046-1afd-4f1a-85fc-8219c79e1504) until you are confident that the account is no longer compromised.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="dd805-111">Untuk meminimalkan potensi pelanggaran data dari akun yang dibobol di masa depan, kami sarankan untuk membaca [artikel praktik terbaik Keamanan Office 365](https://support.office.com/article/9295e396-e53d-49b9-ae9b-0b5828cdedc3).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dd805-111">To minimize the potential of a data breach or a compromised account in the future, we recommend reading our [Office 365 Security best practices article](https://support.office.com/article/9295e396-e53d-49b9-ae9b-0b5828cdedc3).</span></span>
